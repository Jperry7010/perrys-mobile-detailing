# Perry's Mobile Detailing
## Project Charter

Version: 1.0

Created: July 2026

---

# Vision

Build the most trusted and professional mobile detailing experience in San Antonio.

Perry's Mobile Detailing exists to provide premium automotive detailing directly at the customer's home or workplace while delivering exceptional customer service, transparent pricing, and an effortless booking experience.

This website is not a school project.

It is the digital headquarters of Perry's Mobile Detailing.

---

# Mission

Every customer should be able to:

• Learn about our services in under two minutes.

• Book an appointment in under two minutes.

• Feel confident they are hiring professionals.

• Receive excellent communication before, during, and after every appointment.

---

# Core Values

## Professionalism

Every interaction should build trust.

Customers should always feel respected.

---

## Quality

Every vehicle deserves premium attention regardless of value.

Whether it is a family SUV, a work truck, a luxury sedan, or a classic muscle car, every customer receives the same high standard of care.

---

## Convenience

Our customers should never have to waste their time driving to a detail shop.

We bring premium detailing directly to their driveway.

---

## Honesty

Transparent pricing.

No hidden fees.

No surprises.

---

## Community

Perry's Mobile Detailing is a local San Antonio business dedicated to serving neighbors with integrity and professionalism.
