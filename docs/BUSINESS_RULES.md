## Professional Detailer Profiles

Each Professional Detailer profile should include:

- Professional photo
- Bio
- Years of experience
- Services completed
- Customer rating
- Background checked status
- Certifications, if applicable
- Assigned vehicle
- ETA for appointment arrival

## Booking Flow

Book  
→ Choose service  
→ Choose time  
→ Enter address  
→ Payment  
→ Appointment Confirmed

Payment is required before an appointment is confirmed.
